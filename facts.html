Interesting facts learned from using this app

  • Of all the countries and states listed, Mexico has by far the largest percent of young people. Over half the population is under 30.

  • 1/3 of the total population of the United States was under 12 when 9/11 happened.


  • People around 85 years old today were about 12 when Bambi came out.
  • People around 30 years old today were about 12 when the Nintendo 64 was released.
  • About half of the population of China today was 12 or older when the World Wide Web was released.
