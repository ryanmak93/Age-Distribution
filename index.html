Still Crazy After All These Years

This is my application for displaying the age distribution in America as a whole and in each state. It also has information on our neighbors to the north and south and a few Asian countries. You can use it to see the age distribution of 3 states or countries.

You can see the population for each age from 1 to 85, or in intervals of 10. You can also select an event to see how much of the population was at a conscious age at the time of the event.
